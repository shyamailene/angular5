Let's talk about Shakti-US

SHAKTI-US works towards advancement of equity for women through advocacy, education, better healthcare and economic empowerment. SHAKTI-US aims to achieve its goal of championing the advancement of women by providing, developing and promoting leadership in women, through professional expertise, mentoring and through building strategic alliances with partners in the areas of sustainable rural development. The goal of SHAKTI-US is to ensure that the underprivileged women in rural communities are empowered with the right skills and tools that enable them to be the agents of change in overall human development.

The Millennium Development Goals set forth by the United Nations at the turn of the new millennium did help the cause of gender equality and women’s empowerment throughout the developing nations. Unfortunately, the initiatives for gender equality have not fully translated into gender equity in terms of access to education, healthcare and economic opportunities for women in rural and under developed regions of the world. Violence and discrimination on women have not only been common but on the rise in many countries across the world.

We at SHAKTI-US believe that gender equality is a fundamental human right and women empowerment is a necessary condition for an equitable, peaceful, and prosperous world. We believe that achieving sustainable development through women empowerment in rural and under developed areas in the areas of education, healthcare and economic opportunities would uplift the condition and dignity of under privileged women.

SHAKTI-US will strive to mentor and groom the younger generation of women to actively participate in the socio-political and economic decision-making processes through various programs in education, healthcare and economic development using Information and Communication Technologies.

SHAKTI-US would like to bring the underprivileged women to the forefront on the world stage so that they can help build sustainable and stable societies.



Spirit of Human Acceptance

Gender equity is not a privilege it’s a right. SHAKTI-US aims to promote neutrality between all genders, while celebrating distinctive norms and traditions of cultures world over.

Knowledge, Talent

SHAKTI-US enables women with the means and opportunities to showcase their abilities to reach the epitome of their potential. SHAKTI-US aims to empower women by promoting programs and activities in education, healthcare and economic development.

Insight

SHAKTI-US specifically caters to rural and underprivileged woman the world over. Shakti strives to Expand choices for women and help them make informed decisions in life.

Let's talk about us

Gowri Vemuri

President

Gowri Vemuri was born in Hyderabad, India blessed with two children Residing with her beautiful family in Virginia, USA since 1998.She holds master’s degrees in Mathematics and Geo-Physics and works as senior software engineer. Her passion in STEM, lead her to coach and mentor, kids through various voluntary programs throughout USA.

Gowri is a strong believer in “Service to humanity is Service to God”. Her parents played a big role in imparting the “service to humanity” from a very young age.

She is the founding member of SHAKTI organization, which is aimed at Social and Economic Empowerment for women and girls. Her passion, strength in Education and service will bring the needed strength and people to the organization. SHAKTI is not only an empowering organization, but also one that underlines the value of education and health for improving the lives of women and girls who are our future generation.

Raksha Pothapragada

Youth President

Raksha Pothapragada was born in Hyderabad, Telangana, India and moved to America in 2002.She is an undergrad studying Biochemistry and Psychology at University of Pittsburgh. She aspires to go to medical school and ultimately provide health care treatment for those less fortunate. She has been involved in various community services from a young age.

Currently serving as a communications chair for Pitt Global Brigades program and also as a fundraising chair for Imagination project. For next year she will be serving as Vice-President for Imagination project and Campus Chairperson for Global Brigades.

She is an advocate for equal rights and she hopes for SHAKTI to be an initiative that help women get on an equal platform as men. Raksha believes that in order for someone to be completely self-reliant and self-empowered, that person needs to have a strong belief system to support it. Education and Empowerment forms the backbone of this belief system. So, Raksha hopes to help SHAKTI grow and work on improving women empowerment through various channels.
